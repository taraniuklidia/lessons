About the house owners

In the heart of Brambly Hedge, nestled beneath a tangle of wild roses and honeysuckle,
was the cozy home of the Fieldmouse family. It was a quaint little house made from twigs,
leaves, and woven grass, and it was perfectly tailored to the needs of its tiny inhabitants:
Mr. and Mrs. Fieldmouse and their three young ones, Daisy, Pip, and Tumble.

In Brambly Hedge, a house so small,
Made of twigs, leaves, and dreams that call.
With lantern's light in the kitchen's nook,
And in cozy bedroom where dreams are forsook.

Pantry stocked with treasures kept,
Nuts and seeds, carefully swept.
In the garden, swings on the old oak tree,
A world of joy for the Fieldmouse family.

In this small house, love's sweet embrace,
In every corner, a cherished place.
Brambly Hedge, where dreams do dwell,
A tiny home, but its stories, they swell.
